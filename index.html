About Us

Building Future Leaders Through Sport Since 1996

Whitehead Vultures Academy of Sports is a Christian-founded, community-based organization located in the North West District of Harare. We use sport as a catalyst for development, evangelism, and health education.

Our programs reach youth in Mabelreign, Tynwald, Chitungwiza, Ruwa, Marondera and Norton – providing structured training while instilling values of teamwork, discipline, respect, and integrity.

Our Mission

To develop holistic athletes through sport, education, and Christian values, using athletics as a vehicle to combat drug abuse and promote healthy lifestyles.

Our Vision

To be Zimbabwe's leading academy producing disciplined leaders who excel in sport, academics, and community service across Harare and beyond.

Basketball

Volleyball & Netball

Community Impact

Sport as a Tool for Transformation

Since 1996, we've used athletics to address real community challenges: rising substance abuse, school dropouts, and lack of positive youth engagement in Harare's high-density suburbs.

Our themed tournaments bring together health professionals, pastors, and community leaders for workshops on HIV/AIDS, drug abuse prevention, mental health, and leadership.

  • 1

    Health Education

    Regular workshops facilitated by health authorities and community leaders.

  • 2

    Life Skills

    Discipline, time management, communication, and teamwork on and off the field.

  • 3

    Pathways

    Connecting talented players to schools, clubs, and development tournaments.
